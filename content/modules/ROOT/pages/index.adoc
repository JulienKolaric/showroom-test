This guide helps presenters deliver a friendly, fun demonstration of AI on Red Hat OpenShift {ocp_version}.

== What This Demo Covers

This demonstration shows how OpenShift turns AI from a science project into something your teams can actually ship:

* A unified platform for models, notebooks, and inference
* Self-service access for developers without waiting on a GPU ticket queue
* Enterprise guardrails baked in, not bolted on later
* A clear path from experiment to production on the same cluster
